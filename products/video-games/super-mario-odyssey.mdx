Set off on a globe-trotting adventure with Mario and his new companion, Cappy! In Super Mario Odyssey, you’ll explore a variety of imaginative kingdoms, from bustling cityscapes to mysterious ruins and lush forests, each filled with secrets, surprises, and unique challenges.

Use Mario’s new capture ability to take control of objects, enemies, and even vehicles, opening up creative ways to solve puzzles and reach hidden areas. Collect Power Moons to fuel your airship, the Odyssey, and unlock new destinations as you race to stop Bowser’s wedding plans.

Enjoy inventive platforming, charming characters, and a joyful soundtrack that brings each world to life. Play solo or team up with a friend in local co-op, where one player controls Mario and the other controls Cappy. With countless collectibles, costumes, and mini-games, Super Mario Odyssey offers endless fun for players of all ages and skill levels.
